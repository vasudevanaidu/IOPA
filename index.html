about us

India's premier association dedicated to advancing education and clinical practice in orofacial pain

Established in March 2025, the Indian Orofacial Pain Association (IOPA) is India's premier association dedicated to advancing education and clinical practice in orofacial pain management. Born out of a shared vision to address the growing need for excellence in this specialized field, IOPA strives to improve awareness, elevate patient care, and empower professionals to tackle the challenges of orofacial pain effectively.

Defining Orofacial Pain

Orofacial Pain The 12thSpeciality

Orofacial pain is more than just discomfort—it's a complex challenge affecting the face, mouth, and jaw, from Temporomandibular disorders to Neuropathic pain.
Recognized as the 12th dental speciality in the U.S. in 2020, it now gets the expert attention it deserves, bringing precision care and lasting relief to those who need it most.
